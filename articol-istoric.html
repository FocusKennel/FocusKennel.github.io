The Story of Focus Kennel

de Cristian DragomirFondator · Medic veterinar · București, România

After I saw for the first time a Dobermann, I knew this was the dog I wanted to own. The Dobermann impresses me by his athletic structure, firm expression, joyfulness, his great capacity to work and make a team with his owner.

Being fascinated by this breed, when I was 16 — in 1984 — I bought my first Dobe. At the time, this meant a big financial effort for me, but it was all worth it. Droll was not a successful show dog but he was excellent as a work dog. Unfortunately he developed liver cancer and died tragically at 7.

From this first experience I learned a lot about this breed, dog shows and work trials.

1994 — The Beginning

In 1994 I decided to breed, so I imported two females with a very good bloodline: Doriental Loren and Blackness Bessy — daughter of Jaron Royal Bell and granddaughter of Tor Bay de Amiluc. This was the beginning of the Focus Kennel.

1997 — Graaf Tila raises the bar

In 1997 I imported a beautiful female puppy, Vana Fortobella, and a three-year-old male: Graaf Tila v. Neerlands Stam.

Tila proved to be an excellent work dog — although we started training quite late. He passed the BH exam with 59 points out of 60 and scored Excellent at the SchH I exam with 271 points, including a perfect 100 for the attack phase. Tila was also a very good show dog, winning in Romania, Yugoslavia, Croatia, Hungary, Greece, Bulgaria and Ukraine.

Graaf Tila v. Neerlands Stam — Mid and East European Champion 1999 · 2nd in Champion Class at the European Dog Show, Poznań 2000.

His offspring are successful in shows: Focus Olla, Focus Orson, Focus Ozzy, Focus Ono, Focus Cardinal, Focus Castor, Focus Born to be Wild, Focus Bohemian Rhapsody — many Rpj, CAC, CACIB, and some became Romanian Champions.

Building the line

In 2001, a friend of mine, George Bundan, brought to Romania a very good show female, Tom-Dober Elli, and we decided to include her in the kennel.

Besides Tila, I used for breeding some very important studs:

  • Icaro di Villa Castelli
  • Baron Nike Renewal
  • Velenberg Julfil Dastin
  • Axel di Casa Ravasi
  • Casanova Altobello
  • Tamerlan iz Slavnoi Stai
  • Gino Gomez del Citone

Some of the pups that resulted became winning show dogs: Focus Sonya (Romanian Junior Champion, Derby Winner 2000) and Focus Honeymoon (Derby Winner 2002). Others were still young but very promising.

Dobermanns born at the Focus Kennel are now living in all parts of the world.

Philosophy

In my opinion a good Dobermann should be more than just an anatomically correct show dog. He must be, first of all, well balanced and sure in his responses — a reliable guardian and a lovable family dog.

The passion for Dobermanns made me travel a lot and make friends all over Europe. My hobby also brought me close to some very special people who share my love for this breed and work together with me.

„A good Dobermann should be well balanced and sure in his responses — a reliable guardian and a lovable family dog."
— Cristian Dragomir
