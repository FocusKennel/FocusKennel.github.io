    02 — Science & Genetics

    The numbers behind the breed

    A lab interface for the things that decide a Dobermann's life — heart, hips, genetic diversity. Selection at Focus is a measurement, not a hope.

    CardiacDCM-001
    0

    Lifetime DCM prevalence

    Roughly 3 in 5 Dobermanns develop dilated cardiomyopathy. We require annual Holter + echo on breeding stock — the single most important screen in the breed.

    PopulationDIV-002
    0

    Countries with Focus dogs

    A wide, deliberately managed gene flow. Diversity is selected for, not stumbled into — every pairing is scored for relatedness before it happens.

    SelectionIDX-003
    0

    Traits in the selection index

    Work, health, conformation, temperament, fertility, diversity — weighted together. A title alone is a weak, distorted genetic signal.

    GeneticsCOI-004

    Inbreeding coefficient (COI) over a pairing

    Lower is healthier. We model the coefficient of inbreeding before breeding — the goal is genetic distance, not a famous name repeated on both sides of the pedigree.
